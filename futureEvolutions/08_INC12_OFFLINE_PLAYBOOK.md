**For:** Jurgen / AVYclima
**Window:** ~6–8 weeks elapsed, ~12–18 hours of your own time spread across that window
**Why offline:** every item below requires you to authenticate as the business owner, upload proof documents, or click verification links sent to your email. None of it can be automated from inside a coding session.

## How to sequence this

There's a reason to do these in order rather than in parallel — earlier items unlock or strengthen later ones:

```
Week 1     Google Business Profile (everything else builds on this)
              │
Week 1–2   NAP cleanup on Companyweb, Gouden Gids, Kompass, LinkedIn
              │   (must match the live site exactly: 9472, founded 2024,
              │    +32 472 65 76 47, info@avyclima.be)
              │
Week 2–4   Daikin dealer locator + RESCert / certification listings
              │   (manufacturer/cert authority — high-signal links)
              │
Week 4–8   Review acquisition flow + Homeproved + Trustpilot
              (only after GBP and NAP are stable)
```

If you skip ahead — say, you set up Trustpilot before NAP is consistent — you'll have to redo paperwork later. Resist that urge.

## 1. Google Business Profile (Week 1, ~2 hours then 5–14 days waiting)

**Why it's first:** GBP is the local-search anchor. Local Services Ads, Maps visibility, and Knowledge Panel info all key off it. Nothing else in this list compounds without it.

**Before you start, have ready:**
- AVYclima's official email address (info@avyclima.be — match the site)
- Your phone (+32 472 65 76 47)
- Postal address: AVYclima, 9472 Denderleeuw, België
- 4–8 install photos (use the ones in `images/installations/` — they're already on the site)
- Logo file (`images/logo.png`)
- Founder photo if you have one

**Steps:**
1. Go to https://business.google.com/create
2. Choose "Yes, I deliver goods and services to my customers" → service-area business (no storefront)
3. Service area: list **Aalst, Denderleeuw, Ninove, Haaltert, Erpe-Mere, Lede, Dendermonde**, plus **Liedekerke, Affligem, Herzele, Zottegem, Geraardsbergen** if you want broader reach. Match this to your `werkgebied.html`.
4. Primary category: **Airconditioning Contractor** (HVAC contractor in EN). Secondary: **Heat pump installer**, **Heating contractor**.
5. Hours: Ma–Vr 8:00–18:00, Za 9:00–13:00. Match `contact.html` exactly.
6. Description (750 char max): use a tightened version of `over-ons.html`'s pitch. Avoid keyword stuffing — Google penalises that. Mention Daikin specialisatie, founded 2024, and the kernregio.
7. Upload photos: logo + 4–8 install photos + cover image. Add geo-tags if your camera saved them.
8. **Verification:** Google sends a postcard (5–14 days) to a physical address, or in some cases offers phone/email verification. For a service-area business at a residential address, postcard is most common.

**"Done" looks like:** GBP shows verified status, your business appears in Google Maps when you search "AVYclima Denderleeuw" from a logged-out browser, and the Knowledge Panel on the right when you Google your name shows hours + phone + website.

**Common pitfalls:**
- Don't list the same business under multiple addresses — it gets flagged as spam.
- Don't use the residential address as a public street address; service-area businesses should hide the street.
- Once verified, **don't change category or NAP** for at least 60 days — repeated edits trigger re-review.

## 3. Manufacturer + certification authority (Week 2–4, ~3 hours)

These are the high-signal external links the audit specifically mentioned as Daikin-specialist trust drivers.

### 3a. Daikin Belgian dealer locator
URL: https://www.daikin.be/nl_be/dealerlocator.html → "Word een Daikin partner" / partner application
- Submit AVYclima as a registered Daikin dealer
- You'll need to provide: VAT number, koeltechnisch bekwaamheidscertificaat, optionally Daikin training certificates
- Review can take 2–4 weeks
- **Outcome:** AVYclima appears on the official Daikin dealer locator + a manufacturer link from daikin.be → avyclima.be (very high SEO authority)

### 3b. RESCert installer registry
URL: https://www.rescert.be
- Check eligibility: RESCert certification is for installers of renewable-energy systems (heat pumps, solar boilers). Required for clients to claim certain Vlaamse subsidies.
- If you don't already hold RESCert: enrol in the certification process. There's an exam fee and a renewal cycle.
- If you do hold it: ensure your AVYclima profile is listed publicly.
- **Outcome:** You appear on the official RESCert installer search, plus you become eligible for clients' subsidies that require a RESCert installer (which is a competitive lever, not just an SEO one).

### 3c. Vlaams Energieagentschap (VEA) registries
- VEA maintains lists of certified installers per technology
- Check if you're listed for warmtepompen and solar boilers; correct any outdated entries

### 3d. Optional but cheap: Bouwunie / Confederatie Bouw
- Sector federations sometimes maintain searchable member directories
- Membership comes with cost; only worth it if you'd join for the broader benefits (legal advice, training)

**"Done" looks like:** at least one inbound link from daikin.be or rescert.be to avyclima.be. Verify with a `link:avyclima.be` Google search a few weeks after submission.

## 4. Review acquisition flow (Week 4–8 and onwards)

**Don't start before** GBP is verified and NAP cleanup is complete. Reviews on the wrong listing are nearly impossible to migrate.

### 4a. The post-install ask
Build this into your standard handover routine after every completed installation:

1. After oplevering, send the customer an email within 24–48 hours thanking them and asking for a review. Keep it short (3 sentences) and personal.
2. Include a **direct link** to your GBP review form. Get this from your GBP dashboard → "Share review form".
3. For older satisfied customers: a one-time outreach email asking for a review is acceptable — but only to genuine satisfied customers.

### 4b. Email template (Dutch)
Save this somewhere; you'll reuse it monthly.

> Beste [klant],
>
> Bedankt om voor AVYclima te kiezen voor de installatie van uw [airco / warmtepomp]. Het was fijn werken en ik hoop dat u tevreden bent over het resultaat.
>
> Mag ik u een kleine gunst vragen? Een korte review op Google helpt enorm om andere mensen in [gemeente] te tonen wat ze kunnen verwachten. Het kost u 1 minuut: [direct GBP review link].
>
> Bij voorbaat dank,
> Arne — AVYclima

### 4c. QR code op factuur
- Generate a QR code that points to your GBP review URL
- Print it bottom-right on every paper factuur or include it in PDF facturen
- One-time setup, pays off forever

### 4d. Target trajectories
| Time | Realistic target |
|---|---|
| Month 1 (after GBP verified) | 2–3 reviews |
| Month 3 | 8–10 reviews |
| Month 6 | 15–25 reviews |
| Month 12 | 40+ reviews |

If you hit 10 verified Google reviews with a 4.5+ average, you'll outrank most competitors in your kernregio for "airco installateur Aalst" and similar queries.

### 4e. Other review platforms (lower priority)
- **Homeproved.be** — Belgian construction-focused, domain-relevant, modest traffic
- **Trustpilot** — broader audience but less HVAC-specific
- **Facebook reviews** — only if you maintain an active Facebook page

Don't spread thin. GBP first to 10+ reviews; *then* maybe Homeproved as a second channel. Trustpilot can wait.

### 4f. Responding to reviews
Always reply, within 5 working days. Templates:

- **Positive:** "Bedankt [voornaam], het was fijn werken aan uw [project] in [gemeente]. Veel comfort gewenst!"
- **Negative:** Acknowledge, don't argue publicly, offer to follow up offline. The public reply is for *future readers*, not for the reviewer.

## 6. What you do *not* need to do

A few things sometimes recommended for HVAC firms that aren't worth the time at your stage:

- **Yelp Belgium** — negligible traffic, not worth claiming.
- **Facebook ads** before GBP is producing leads — burning budget on a cold audience.
- **Gespecialiseerde HVAC-fora link-building** — historic SEO tactic, mostly noise now and risks looking spammy.
- **Press releases** about a small business launch — won't get picked up by Belgian press, expensive for the value.
- **Buying reviews** — Google detects this, your account gets suspended, recovery takes months. Never.
